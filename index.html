What’s happening

Here’s the part most people don’t realize:

Progress doesn’t feel like progress while it’s happening.

You expect fast feedback and clear direction. But real progress is slow, uneven, and hard to see in the moment. It is about continuation, not instant results.

Most people quit here — not because they failed, but because they can’t see that it’s working yet.
